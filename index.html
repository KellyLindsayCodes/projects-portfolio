Happy Hounds

The Brief

Happy Hounds commissioned a website to highlight its primary services in a tone that stayed true to its values. The issue was striking a balance between showcasing the brand's playful nature in combination with its commitment to quality of care. The website design incorporates oranges and yellows to align with the brand's existing, fun colour palette, but is balanced by white to signify section breaks. The website also has a dedicated reviews section that is supported by the brand's photography to further instil feelings of joy and care, and reinforce the brand's esteemed reputation.

Many a Meal

The Brief

Many a Meal is an application that establishes a connection between users and an extensive database of recipes by leveraging an external API. The app features a blog-like aesthetic design and is equipped with a dynamic search engine, ensuring a seamless, user-friendly, and swift experience. The landing page is thoughtfully organized, presenting users with four distinct recipe collections, along with dedicated sections for popular recipes and vegetarian options.
